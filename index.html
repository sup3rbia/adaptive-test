Honor Candy’s

•EST 1957

Honor Candy was started in 1957 by a small group of Berber farmers producing the best, most delicious and most healthy cane sugar from the golden fields of Tunisia. In 1968 Honor Candy opened its first factory in Hammamet, Tunisia. This factory produced only caramel dippers and licorice ropes at that time. Today, our company operates 5 factories across the globe and produces more than 130 products including candies, chocolate bars and drinking chocolates.
